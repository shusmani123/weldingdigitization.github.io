About SmartWeld

SmartWeld positions research-backed welding digitization as a practical solution pathway for robotic welding, process visibility, and adaptive control.

What we offer

Welding Digitization Solution

  • Closed-loop robotic welding studies and pilot support
  • Weld monitoring and defect-aware process insight
  • AI-assisted interpretation of weld-quality conditions
  • Multi-sensor monitoring for surface and internal defect analysis
  • Research-to-pilot collaboration for industrial and academic users

Commercialization pathway

A simple roadmap helps present the platform as solution-oriented while remaining realistic for a university-based offering.

1. Website Presence Present the solution, platforms, publications, and contact path through a professional digital front door.
2. Capability Packaging Prepare brochures, application notes, demo videos, and visual material for external stakeholders.
3. Partner Discussions Engage prospective industrial partners for pilot dialogue, technical review, and opportunity mapping.
4. Pilot Validation Offer small collaborative trials for selected welding problems and monitoring studies.
5. Funding Support Seek external support to mature the research prototype toward a stronger technology prototype.
6. Transfer Path Move toward service contracts, licensing, or another university-approved commercialization route.
